BIRYANI

Ingredients:

For the Chicken Marinade:

  1. 500 grams (about 1 lb) chicken, cut into pieces
  2. 1 cup yogurt
  3. 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  4. 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  5. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  6. Salt to taste
  7. Juice of 1 lemon
  8. 2 tablespoons cooking oil

    9. For the Rice:

  9. 2 cups basmati rice
  10. Water for soaking rice
  11. 4-5 cups water for cooking rice
  12. 4-5 green cardamom pods
  13. 4-5 cloves
  14. 2-inch cinnamon stick
  15. Salt to taste
